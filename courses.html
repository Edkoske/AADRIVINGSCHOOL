Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses

How long does it take to learn to drive?

The duration varies based on the course you choose and your learning pace. The Beginner course typically takes 2-4 weeks, while the Intensive course can be completed in 2 weeks.

Do I need my own car for lessons?

No, we provide well-maintained training vehicles equipped with dual controls for your safety. You can choose between manual or automatic transmission.

What is the minimum age to start driving lessons?

You can start learning to drive at 17 years old in Kenya. For learner's permit, you need to be at least 17 years old.

Do you offer pick-up and drop-off services?

Yes, we offer complimentary pick-up and drop-off services within Nairobi metropolitan area for all our training sessions.

What happens if I need to reschedule a lesson?

You can reschedule your lesson at least 24 hours in advance without any charges. Contact your instructor or our office to arrange a new time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, M-Pesa, and credit/debit cards. We also offer flexible payment plans for our comprehensive courses.
