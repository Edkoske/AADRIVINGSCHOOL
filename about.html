About Us

Discover our story and meet the team behind your driving success

Your Trusted Driving School Since 2012

AA Driving School was founded with a simple mission: to make road safety a priority and help people become confident, responsible drivers. What started as a small operation in Nairobi has grown into one of Kenya's most trusted driving schools.

Over the years, we've helped over 5,000 students achieve their driving goals, with a remarkable 98% pass rate on first attempts. Our commitment to quality training and student success has made us a leader in the industry.
